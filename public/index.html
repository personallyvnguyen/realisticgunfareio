Know this

  • Fair, even battle. Both sides field the same battalion of 5-man fire-teams, each led by its own leader. Wipe the enemy to win — no respawns.
  • Your crosshair is your cone of fire — it opens up with range, sway, movement and fatigue. Point-blank you hit exactly where you point; aim down sights (R-Click) to tighten it.
  • Stance matters: prone/crouch = lower profile, steadier aim, hide behind low cover. Prone with a bipod weapon is rock-steady.
  • Bodies can lie. A wounded enemy may play dead and ambush you — real corpses pool blood, fakers don't. Friendly fire is on: mind your lanes.
  • Vehicles need a driver + gunner — drive and an ally mans the gun; enemies crew idle rigs too. Small arms barely scratch a tank.
  • Drones last a while and rearm/recharge if you fly them back over your body.
